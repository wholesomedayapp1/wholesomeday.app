Privacy Policy for Wholesome Day

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

Welcome to Wholesome Day. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

Location Data: Wholesome Day temporarily accesses your device's geolocation data solely to fetch local weather forecasts and regional news updates. We do not track, log, sell, or share your location history with third-party tracking networks.

Personal Data: Your journal entries, expense tracking logs, and hydration inputs are securely processed and stored via our encrypted cloud backend (Supabase) and are tied strictly to your secure User ID. This data is private and never shared with third parties.

2. Data Deletion

You have full ownership of your data. You may delete your account and instantly wipe all personal logs from our cloud databases at any time using the "Delete Account" button within the app settings.

3. Security

We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your sensitive information during transmission and storage.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: wholesomedayapp@gmail.com
